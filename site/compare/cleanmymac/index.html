Binky vs CleanMyMac

CleanMyMac is MacPaw's flagship cleaner — scans, uninstalls, maintenance scripts, malware checks. Binky doesn't polish the whole machine; it babysits the noisy folder until everything lands where you said it should.

Choose CleanMyMac for periodic deep cleans

When you want reclaim-disk workflows, app removal, and scheduled tune-ups from one dashboard.

Choose Binky for live arrivals

When files keep landing in Downloads (or elsewhere) and you want deterministic routing, Review, and MIT code.
