Binky vs Refolder

Refolder lives on Setapp and leans on AI for naming and sorting. Binky leans on file types, patience, and a Review pile when the Mac isn’t sure.

Choose Refolder for AI-driven naming and sorting

When you already live in Setapp, want intelligent cleanup, and batch renaming with Apple Intelligence–style help sounds right.

Choose Binky for free, rule-free, no subscription

When you want predictable sorted folders, open source, stable-file detection, and no monthly receipt — just a quieter Downloads folder.
